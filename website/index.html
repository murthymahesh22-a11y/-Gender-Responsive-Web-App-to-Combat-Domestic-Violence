Gender-Responsive Web App to Combat Domestic Violence

Purpose

Help people facing domestic violence by offering resources and support.

Features

  • Information on legal rights
  • Support services directory (hotlines, shelters, counselling)
  • Safe ways to seek help (discreet tools, emergency contacts)

Goal

Promote gender equality and address health risks linked with abuse.

App User Roles & Main Functions

Admin
Manages content, users, and data security.
Victim / Survivor
Accesses resources, seeks help, connects with support.
Counsellor
Provides guidance and tracks progress.
Legal Advisor
Shares legal advice and updates legal resources.

Social Impact of the App

  • Empowers victims with easy access to help and protection.
  • Raises awareness about rights and health resources.
  • Creates a safe, inclusive space to fight domestic violence in communities.
